Know the brand

Brand name: Alpenliebe
Launched in 1995. This brand has served about 30 countries till now.The parent company that owns this brand is Perfettivanmelle which is serving costumers since 2001 with various other tasty candy brands like Mentos, Centerfruit and Chupa Chups circulating in over 150 countries worldwide.

Secret of Sweetness

The Cream Strawberry flavour name is from the cream, which indicates the milk cream. The cream and strawberry flavour are used to enhance the flavour and taste which is mentioned in this line.

Ingredients
Sugar, Liquid Glucose, Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil, Sweetened Condensed Milk (1.6%), Stabiliser (INS 1405), Medium Fat Cream (1.3%), Acidity Regulators (INS 270, INS 325, INS 500(ii)), Emulsifier (INS 322), Edible Common Salt (Iodized), Strawberry Fruit Pulp (0.26%), Humectant (INS 422). CONTAINS PERMITTED SYNTHETIC FOOD COLOURS (INS 124, INS 110) AND ADDED NATURE-IDENTICAL FLAVOURING SUBSTANCES (STRAWBERRY).
